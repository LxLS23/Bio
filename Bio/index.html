Biography

Leonor


She was born on October 23, 2000, in Valmiera, Latvia.

Leonor had a wonderful childhood surrounded by love and joy. She grew up in a caring family and was always known for her kindness and creativity. Since her early years, she showed a special interest in fashion and style, often designing small accessories and experimenting with colors and fabrics.

As she grew older, Leonor’s passion for design became stronger. She decided to study fashion design, where she learned about modern trends, sustainability, and the importance of expressing identity through clothing. Her work often reflects her personality — elegant, bold, and full of life.

Beyond her professional interests, Leonor enjoys spending time with her friends, traveling, and discovering new cultures. Her goal is to inspire others to embrace their individuality through creativity and confidence.
